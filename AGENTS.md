# AGENTS.md - Educational Slides Project

## Project Overview
- **Purpose**: Create English teaching slides from 6 academic abstracts for Taiwanese university students (EFL learners)
- **Format**: Reveal.js HTML presentations
- **Output**: 6 HTML files hosted on GitHub Pages

## Source Files
- `abstract1.txt` ~ `abstract6.txt` - Academic paper abstracts (EdTech/AI in Education)

## Generated Slides
- `abstract1.html` - Generative AI & Creative Thinking Learning
- `abstract2.html` - Multimodal Learning Analytics (MMLA) Systematic Review
- `abstract3.html` - VR Serious Games & Environmental Education
- `abstract4.html` - AI Knowledge Scale Development & Validation
- `abstract5.html` - Critical Thinking Intervention & GenAI Collaborative Learning
- `abstract6.html` - AGI & Discourse Analysis (CGT-LLM Framework)

## Slide Structure (per abstract)
1. Title slide (English title + Chinese translation)
2. Highlights (research key points)
3. Vocabulary (technical terms with Chinese definitions)
4. Grammar focus
5. Abstract content (full English sentences with Chinese translations)
6. Summary
