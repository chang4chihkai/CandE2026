Measuring different types and domains of AI knowledge: Developing and validating a performance-based scale

測量不同類型與領域的AI知識：開發與驗證表現本位量表

Highlights 研究亮點

  • A novel two-axial framework for AI knowledge, including knowledge types and domains.
    提出AI知識的新穎雙軸框架，包含知識類型與知識領域。
  • A performance-based scale, theoretically grounded, validated with 800 adults.
    一個以理論為基礎的表現本位量表，以800名成人驗證。
  • The objective assessment addresses gaps in current AI literacy measurement.
    客觀評量填補了當前AI素養測量的缺口。
  • Epistemic and society-related knowledge negatively correlate with trust in GenAI.
    認識論與社會相關知識與對GenAI的信任呈負相關。
  • The two-axial assessment provides nuanced insights into AI and GenAI knowledge.
    雙軸評量提供對AI與GenAI知識的細緻洞察。

Abstract 內容（一）

  • As artificial intelligence (AI) and generative AI (GenAI) technologies become increasingly integrated into everyday life, the need for validated tools that measure people's knowledge about AI grows.
    隨著AI與生成式AI技術越來越融入日常生活，對有效測量人們AI知識的工具需求也日益增長。
  • Here, we present the development and validation of a theoretically driven, performance-based scale for assessing AI and GenAI knowledge.
    本研究呈現一個以理論驅動、表現本位的量表，用於評估AI與GenAI知識的開發與驗證。
  • The scale is grounded in a two-axial framework.
    該量表基於雙軸框架。
  • One axis captures three knowledge types: content knowledge (what AI is and where it is encountered), procedural knowledge (how AI systems operate and are used), and epistemic knowledge (what features and construction processes characterize AI outputs).
    第一軸包含三種知識類型：內容知識（AI是什麼、在哪裡遇到）、程序性知識（AI系統如何運作與使用）、認識論知識（AI輸出的特徵與建構過程）。

Abstract 內容（二）

  • The other axis encompasses three knowledge domains: technology-related knowledge (AI systems), user-related knowledge (users' interaction with AI), and society-related knowledge (the social and ethical implications of AI).
    第二軸涵蓋三個知識領域：技術相關知識（AI系統）、用戶相關知識（用戶與AI的互動）、社會相關知識（AI的社會與倫理影響）。
  • Based on an online survey of 800 internet-using adults from Israel, the 26-item scale was evaluated using confirmatory factor analysis, which demonstrated an acceptable model fit.
    基於以色列800名網路使用成人的線上調查，26題量表使用驗證性因素分析評估，顯示模型適配度可接受。
  • It was further validated through two-stage structural equation modeling and group comparisons.
    並透過兩階段結構方程模型與團體比較進一步驗證。

Abstract 內容（三）

  • Overall, the scale was found to be both valid and practically insightful: while it reproduces the expected relationships with additional constructs (e.g., trust in GenAI, attitudes toward AI) and expected differences between demographic groups, it also provides nuanced insights on the intricacies of AI knowledge.
    整體而言，該量表被發現既有效又實用：它重現了與額外構念（如對GenAI的信任、對AI的態度）及人口統計團體間預期差異的預期關係，也提供了對AI知識複雜性的細緻洞察。
  • For example, the scale indicates that the relationship between trust in GenAI and knowledge about AI is grounded in both epistemic and societal knowledge.
    例如，量表顯示對GenAI的信任與AI知識之間的關係建立在認識論與社會知識之上。
  • Thus, this novel tool affords more precise investigations into how different types and domains of AI knowledge relate to perceptions, behaviors, and decision-making in an AI-mediated world.
    因此，這個新穎工具能更精確地研究不同類型與領域的AI知識如何與AI中介世界中人們的感知、行與決策相關。

Summary 總結

主要發現：

  • 開發26題雙軸量表（3類型 × 3領域）
  • 800人驗證，模型適配度良好
  • 認識論與社會知識與信任呈負相關
  • 提供AI素養測量的客觀工具
