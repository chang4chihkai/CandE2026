From automation to thinking: The role of AGI in discourse analysis of computer-supported collaborative learning based on computational grounded theory

從自動化到思考：基於計算扎根理論的AGI在CSCL話語分析中的角色

Highlights 研究亮點

  • Proposes a novel CGT-LLM framework for collaborative discourse analysis.
    提出用於協作話語分析的新穎CGT-LLM框架。
  • Effectively identifies meaningful thematic categories from complex dialogue.
    能有效從複雜對話中識別有意義的主題類別。
  • CGT-LLM outperforms BERT baseline in extracting abstract themes.
    CGT-LLM在提取抽象主題方面優於BERT基線。
  • Demonstrates an effective human-AI partnership for qualitative research.
    展示了質性研究中有效的人-AI夥伴關係。

Abstract 內容（一）

  • Analyzing the complex dialogue central to computer-supported collaborative learning is crucial for understanding learning processes, yet remains a significant challenge for educational researchers due to the labor-intensive nature of manual coding and the semantic limitations of traditional computational methods.
    分析電腦支持協作學習中的複雜對話對理解學習過程至關重要，但由於手動編碼的勞動密集特性與傳統計算方法的語義限制，這對教育研究者來說仍是一大挑戰。
  • Recent advancements have highlighted the potential of Large Language Models (LLMs) to move beyond mere automation, demonstrating an ability for inference without task-specific data that is characteristic of artificial general intelligence.
    最近的進展突顯了大型語言模型超越純自動化的潛力，展現了無需任務特定數據進行推理的能力，這是通用人工智慧的特徵。
  • To harness this potential, this study introduced and evaluated a human-AI collaborative framework (CGT-LLM) that integrates LLMs into computational grounded theory.
    為利用此潛力，本研究引入並評估了一個將LLM整合進計算扎根理論的人-AI協作框架（CGT-LLM）。

Abstract 內容（二）

  • Specifically, CGT-LLM focuses on learning analytics for rich discursive data.
    具體而言，CGT-LLM專注於豐富話語數據的學習分析。
  • Applied to dialogue from a climate change collaborative simulation game, the framework was evaluated against a supervised bidirectional encoder representations from transformers (BERT) baseline.
    應用於氣候變遷協作模擬遊戲的對話，該框架以監督式BERT基線進行評估。
  • The performance of the framework approached human expert-level performance in categories related to explicit instructions, numerical data, or direct statements of intent crucial to game objectives, while also demonstrating promising capability in identifying more abstract and less obvious themes.
    該框架在與明確指示、數據或遊戲目標關鍵意圖直接陳述相關的類別中，表現接近人類專家水平，同時也展現了在識別更抽象及較不明顯主題方面的潛力。

Abstract 內容（三）

  • The findings demonstrate that the researcher's role in computational grounded theory remains critical, particularly in exploring data diversity during the discovery phase, and making final interpretive judgments for abstract themes during the classification phase.
    研究發現表明，研究者在計算扎根理論中的角色仍然關鍵，特別是在發現階段探索數據多樣性，以及在分類階段對抽象主題做出最終詮釋判斷。
  • This framework thus positions LLMs as a valuable assistant rather than as a replacement for human expertise, providing educators and researchers with a tool to gain deeper, more scalable insights into collaborative learning processes, and offering potential to inform the design of timely pedagogical interventions.
    因此，該框架將LLM定位為有價值的助手而非人類專業的替代者，為教育者與研究人員提供一個更深入、更可擴展地了解協作學習過程的工具，並為及時教學介入的設計提供可能性。

Summary 總結

主要發現：

  • 提出CGT-LLM人-AI協作框架
  • 在提取抽象主題方面優於BERT
  • LLM是助手而非取代人類專家
  • 可用於教學介入設計
