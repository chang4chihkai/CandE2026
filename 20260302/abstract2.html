A systematic review of multimodal learning analytics in computer-supported collaborative learning

多模態學習分析在電腦支持協作學習中的系統性回顧

Highlights 研究亮點

  • Empirical studies of multimodal learning analytics in CSCL was reviewed.
    回顧了CSCL中多模態學習分析的實證研究。
  • Data analysis methods, multimodal data types, and learning indicators were analyzed.
    分析了數據分析方法、多模態數據類型與學習指標。
  • Four types of relationships between multimodal data and learning indicators were identified.
    識別出多模態數據與學習指標之間的四種關係類型。
  • Effects of the use of multimodal learning analytics in CSCL were examined.
    檢視了多模態學習分析在CSCL中的應用效果。
  • Theoretical, technological, and practical implications were proposed.
    提出了理論、技術與實務上的意涵。

Abstract 內容（一）

  • Multimodal learning analytics (MMLA) has provided new perspectives for computer-supported collaborative learning (CSCL) by capturing multimodal data to explore behavior, social interaction, cognition, regulation, and emotion in CSCL process.
    多模態學習分析透過收集多模態數據，為電腦支持協作學習提供新視角，以探索CSCL過程中的行為、社會互動、認知、調節與情緒。
  • However, there are critical challenges in handling multimodal data in CSCL context, such as multimodal data preprocessing methods, selecting suitable analysis methods and tools, and integrating multi-source, multimodal data to represent learning indicators in CSCL process.
    然而，在CSCL情境中處理多模態數據存在關鍵挑戰，例如多模態數據預處理方法、選擇合適的分析方法與工具，以及整合多來源、多模態數據以呈現CSCL學習指標。
  • To fill these gaps, this systematic review constructed a conceptual framework of MMLA in CSCL and provided an overview of the contexts, multimodal data, indicators, data preprocessing methods, analysis methods, and tools, and effects of MMLA applications in CSCL from 2012 to 2024.
    為填補這些缺口，本系統性回顧建構了CSCL中MMLA的概念框架，並概述了2012至2024年間CSCL中MMLA應用的情境、多模態數據、指標、數據預處理方法、分析方法、工具及效果。
  • One hundred fourteen studies articles were included for the final synthesis.
    114篇研究文章被納入最終綜合分析。

Abstract 內容（二）

  • Results found that: (1) existing studies primarily focused on groups' social interactions in CSCL;
    結果發現：(1) 現有研究主要聚焦於CSCL中的群體社會互動；
  • (2) visual data was commonly adopted in CSCL;
    (2) 視覺數據在CSCL中被廣泛採用；
  • (3) the relationships between multimodal data and learning indicators in CSCL included four types, namely One-to-One, Many-to-One, One-to-Many, and Many-to-Many, with particular emphasis on Many-to-One relationships;
    (3) CSCL中多模態數據與學習指標的關係包含四種類型：一對一、多對一、一對多及多對多，尤其強調多對一關係；
  • (4) the most frequently used data preprocessing method was manual coding and extraction, and the utilization of traditional analysis methods (e.g., statistical analysis) had gradually decreased in CSCL, while advanced analysis techniques (e.g., AI algorithms) were gradually gaining traction but were not yet widely adopted;
    (4) 最常用的數據預處理方法是手動編碼與提取，傳統分析方法（如統計分析）的使用在CSCL中逐漸減少，而先進分析技術（如AI演算法）逐漸受到關注但尚未廣泛採用；

Abstract 內容（三）

  • and (5) the application of MMLA in CSCL had positive effects on both learners and instructors, which primarily help instructors comprehensively understanding the CSCL process.
    (5) MMLA在CSCL中的應用對學習者與教師都有正面效果，主要幫助教師全面理解CSCL過程。
  • Based on the results, this research proposed theoretical, technological, and practical implications to guide future research in the application of MMLA within CSCL contexts.
    根據結果，本研究提出理論、技術與實務意涵，以指導未來在CSCL情境中應用MMLA的研究。

Summary 總結

主要發現：

  • 114篇研究納入分析（2012-2024）
  • 多模態數據與學習指標有四種關係類型
  • 視覺數據最常被使用
  • AI演算法逐漸增加但尚未普及
  • MMLA對教師理解CSCL過程有幫助
