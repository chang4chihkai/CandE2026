Virtual reality serious games for promoting environmental systems thinking and pro-environmental policy support

VR嚴肅遊戲促進環境系統思維與環保政策支持

Highlights 研究亮點

  • We converted an established a desktop serious game focused on environmental science to a VR serious game.
    我們將一款以環境科學為主的電腦嚴肅遊戲轉換為VR嚴肅遊戲。
  • The VR serious game enhanced users' sense of spatial presence and awe compared to other learning formats.
    與其他學習形式相比，VR嚴肅遊戲增強了使用者的空間臨場感與敬畏感。
  • The VR serious game increased feelings of awe, enhancing connection to nature.
    VR嚴肅遊戲增加了敬畏感，增強了與自然的連結。
  • Our hypotheses suggesting serial indirect effects of learning format on pro-environmental policy support were not supported.
    我們假設學習形式對環保政策支持的間接影響並未獲得支持。
  • Exploratory analyses offered evidence for a pathway linking VR serious games to systems thinking and pro-environmental policy support, but the observed effects were very small in magnitude.
    探索性分析提供了VR嚴肅遊戲與系統思維和環保政策支持之間路徑的證據，但觀察到的效應非常微弱。

Grammar 文法重點

1. 過去式 vs. 過去完成式：
• We converted a desktop game to VR（我們轉換了...）
• The hypotheses were not supported（假設未被支持）
2. 複合形容詞：
• pro-environmental policy support（環保政策支持）
• computer-based learning（電腦輔助學習）
3. 現在分詞作補充說明：
• VR (vs other learning formats) led to increases in...（導致...增加）

Abstract 內容（一）

  • Virtual reality (VR) serious games can expose people to environmental processes they would not otherwise experience.
    虛擬現實嚴肅遊戲能使人接觸到原本無法體驗的環境過程。
  • This can make topics in environmental science more concrete to learners, improving learning outcomes and downstream behaviors related to environmental sustainability.
    這能使環境科學主題更具體化，改善學習成果及與環境永續相關的後續行為。
  • In a randomized experiment (N = 189), we examined the effectiveness of a VR serious game designed to teach people about a topic in environmental science—the Critical Zone—by comparing it to a non-VR version of the game and to a static presentation of the same information on a website.
    在一項隨機實驗（N=189）中，我們比較VR嚴肅遊戲、非VR版本及網站靜態簡報三者對於教學環境科學主題——關鍵帶——的有效性。
  • Although the VR serious game promoted greater spatial presence and feelings of awe (which, in turn, translated to feeling more connected with nature), these effects did not translate to improved learning outcomes and pro-environmental policy support as we hypothesized across two separate models.
    雖然VR嚴肅遊戲促進了更強的空間臨場感與敬畏感（進而轉化為與自然更強的連結感），但這些效果在我們的兩個假設模型中並未轉化為更好的學習成果與環保政策支持。

Abstract 內容（二）

  • Yet, exploratory analyses revealed a very small but significant indirect pathway by which the VR serious game promoted systems thinking about the Food-Energy-Water (FEW) nexus and pro-environmental policy support: VR (vs other learning formats) led to increases in a sense of spatial presence, then to perceived learning effectiveness, then to FEW systems thinking, and, finally, to pro-environmental policy support.
    然而，探索性分析揭示了一條非常微弱但顯著的間接路徑：VR嚴肅遊戲透過空間臨場感增加、感知學習效果提升、FEW系統思維，最終到環保政策支持，促進了系統思維與環保政策支持。
  • Our results shed light on the mixed effect of VR and spatial presence on learning outcomes discussed in the wider literature on VR in education.
    我們的結果闡明了VR與空間臨場感對學習成果的混合影響，這是VR教育文獻中討論的議題。
  • Although the original hypotheses were largely unsupported, by exploring and highlighting pathways from learning formats to outcomes, we demonstrate the potential of VR for promoting learning and pro-environmental policy support.
    雖然原始假設大多未被支持，但透過探索與強調從學習形式到成果的路徑，我們展示了VR在促進學習與環保政策支持方面的潛力。

Summary 總結

主要發現：

  • VR遊戲提升空間臨場感與敬畏感
  • 但未直接轉化為學習成效與政策支持
  • 探索性分析發現微弱間接路徑
  • VR有促進環保教育的潛力
