The effects of critical thinking intervention on reliance behaviors, problem-solving quality, and creativity during human-Generative AI collaborative learning

批判性思維干預對人類與生成式AI協作學習中依賴行為、問題解決品質與創造力的影響

Highlights 研究亮點

  • Few studies explore how interventions shape student reliance on Gen AI.
    很少研究探討干預如何影響學生對GenAI的依賴。
  • We designed interventions to foster critical thinking with Gen AI use.
    我們設計了培養批判性思維與GenAI使用的干預措施。
  • Interventions reduced direct adoption of AI-generated content.
    干預減少了直接採用AI生成內容的行為。
  • Intervention groups produced more creative and original solutions.
    干預組產出更具創意與原創性的解決方案。

Abstract 內容（一）

  • As Generative AI becomes increasingly used in various educational contexts, understanding how students engage with these tools during collaborative problem-solving is critical.
    隨著生成式AI在各種教育情境中越來越被廣泛使用，了解學生在協作問題解決中如何使用這些工具至關重要。
  • While prior research suggests that critical thinking is essential in human-AI problem-solving, few studies have examined how instructional interventions, targeting critical thinking, might shape their reliance behaviors and collaborative outcomes.
    雖然先前研究指出批判性思維在人類-AI問題解決中至關重要，但很少有研究探討針對批判性思維的教學干預如何影響他們的依賴行為與協作成果。
  • This study investigates the effects of a critical thinking intervention embedded in a problem-based learning (PBL) environment where students are engaged with Generative AI.
    本研究探討嵌入問題本位學習環境中的批判性思維干預對學生使用生成式AI的影響。
  • The intervention combined strategies that foster critical thinking, including authentic instruction, structured dialogue, and AI-supported peer mentoring, aiming to promote students' thoughtful engagement and improve problem-solving performance.
    干預結合了培養批判性思維的策略，包括真實教學、結構化對話與AI支持的同儕輔導，旨在促進學生的深思熟慮參與並改善問題解決表現。

Abstract 內容（二）

  • Participants (N = 226) were assigned to experimental (with critical thinking interventions) or comparison (without critical thinking interventions) conditions.
    參與者（N=226）被分配到實驗組（有批判性思維干預）或對照組（無批判性思維干預）條件。
  • We used pre- and post-surveys to measure participants' trust, critical thinking, and AI reliance behaviors, and group reports and chat histories to assess their problem-solving quality and creativity.
    我們使用前測與後測問卷測量參與者的信任、批判性思維與AI依賴行為，並使用團體報告與聊天記錄評估他們的問題解決品質與創造力。
  • Results revealed that the intervention did not produce significant improvement in self-reported critical thinking, possibly due to the short intervention duration.
    結果顯示，干預在自我報告的批判性思維上沒有產生顯著改善，可能由於干預時間較短。
  • However, the intervention led to a marginal reduction in students' thoughtless use of Generative AI and significantly reduced the direct adoption of AI-generated content.
    然而，干預導致學生輕率使用生成式AI的情況些微減少，並顯著減少了直接採用AI生成內容的行為。

Abstract 內容（三）

  • Notably, students in the intervention condition produced more creative solutions, demonstrating higher levels of originality and idea density in their group reports.
    值得注意的是，實驗組的學生產出更具創意的解決方案，在團體報告中展現更高程度的原創性與創意密度。
  • These findings suggest that how students use Generative AI is critical, especially when it is almost impossible to control whether they use it or not.
    這些發現表明，學生如何使用生成式AI至關重要，特別是當幾乎無法控制他們是否使用它時。
  • The study highlights the importance of designing interventions that cultivate students' critical thinking to support creative human-AI problem-solving.
    本研究強調設計培養學生批判性思維以支持創意人-AI問題解決的干預措施的重要性。

Summary 總結

主要發現：

  • N=226，實驗組與對照組比較
  • 干預減少直接採用AI內容
  • 干預組產出更具原創性的解決方案
  • 批判性思維教學有助於人-AI協作
